Escape Game Rules

Look around the room and interact with different parts of the room by clicking the red buttons on the right.

Each button will take you to a part of the room or a hint in the room.


Look at the image or buttons of different parts of the room and find its PATTERN/HINT.

Each number hint will correspond to number/numbers. The numbers MAY OR MAY NOT build up to the "escape passcode".

Figure out the passcode to unlock the secret exit out this door.


You can explore in any order you want. But we recommend that you FIRST check out the FRONT DRAWER, and the PAPER on the drawer.
